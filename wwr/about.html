About Us - White Water Rafting Company

Our goal is to provide you with excellent and best experience possible. We have been providing unforgettable experiences for over 30years. We are a family owned and operated business that is dedicated to providing you with the best experience possible. We have a team of experienced tour guides that are dedicated to providing you with excellent services.

Our History

Founded in 1985, our company started as a small family operation with a passion for the river. Over the decades, we've grown into a premier outfitter, offering exciting adventures for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike. Our purpose remains the same: to provide an easy, accessible, and user-friendly experience for all who want to explore the great outdoors.
