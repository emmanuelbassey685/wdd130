History

Founded in 1985, our company started as a small family operation with a passion for the river. Over the decades, we've grown into a premier outfitter, offering exciting adventures for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike. Our purpose remains the same: to provide an easy, accessible, and user-friendly experience for all who want to explore the great outdoors.
