Akwaibom

Akwa-Ibom is a state in the South-South geopolitical zone of Nigeria. It borders Cross River State to the east, Rivers State and Abia State to the west, and north-west, and the Atlantic Ocean to the south. Akwa Ibom comprises of 33 LGA's with its capital Uyo. It is an oil rich state, blessed with divers culture, hospitable people and rich tourist site attractions.
